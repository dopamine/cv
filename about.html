Why design?

I get most excited about things that blur the lines between art and technology. My passion for design stems from the love for these fields, and a strong desire to make things that make sense. Design is neither art nor technology, but the cross-over of these two fields, where the real magic happens.

Why UX?

From personal frustration with technology, if you will. So many products fail because no-one sat down, looked at it end-to-end and did the thinking to make sense of it all. There are a million ways to save the world, large and small. I think this could be my way.
